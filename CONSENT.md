# Consent & data protection

Most participants are EU-based, so **GDPR applies**. This note is the short privacy notice that
accompanies sign-up; ticking the consent box on the claim form confirms you have read it.

> **WG3 must ratify** the final consent text, the named **data controller**, and the PDF
> retention/deletion policy before launch. The text below is the proposed default.

## What we collect

- Your **GitHub handle** and the papers you claim/return/submit (public, in this repo's `claims/`).
- Your **annotated PDFs**, uploaded to a **private, access-controlled store** (Google Form → restricted
  Drive), visible only to the organizers and you.
- At sign-up, via the private Form: your **email + institution** (private; used only to contact you and
  to verify eligibility), and your **attribution choice** for the public board.
- The **rubric scores** organizers assign your reviews (public handle + numbers only — no PDF, no email).

## What we publish

- The **anonymised or attributed** leaderboard and participation data.
- The **extracted, de-PDF'd annotation dataset** (your comment text + location + paper id + modality) as
  the open material for the paper — deposited to OSF/Zenodo (proposed **CC-BY**). This is *your* commentary,
  not the publisher's text; we never publish the copyrighted PDFs.

## You consent to

1. processing your annotations and participation data for the journal club;
2. publishing the anonymised/attributed annotation dataset and ranking;
3. co-authorship use of your contributions if you qualify (see `co-authorship.md`);
4. the retention/deletion policy for your uploaded PDFs.

You may choose **attributed** (your handle shown) or **anonymous** (a stable pseudonym) on the public
board. You can withdraw and request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting the
organizers; withdrawal does not affect already-published aggregate/anonymised materials.

## Data controller & contact

Proposed data controller: the Local Organiser's institution (CSIC) or HES-SO (**to be ratified**).
Contact: the WG3 mailing list / the organizers named on the
[journal club page](https://www.indos-costaction.eu/working-groups/wg3/federated-journal-club).
